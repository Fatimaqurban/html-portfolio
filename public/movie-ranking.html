Top Three Movies Of All Times

- Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

Its one of the best movies made in Bollywood starring my favourite actor Katrina. The plot is based on the concept of YOLO. You Only Live Once.

-Ye Jawani Hai Deewani

This movie is all about a group class mates who choose to go on a trip however Naina, acted by Deepika, was a nerd and wanted to fufill her parents desire always but she take a plunge and without telling her mother goes for the trip which later becomes the best decision of her lifee

-The Legend Of Maula Jatt

The film follows the journey of Maula, a young man from rural Punjab, as he seeks revenge for his father's death and rises as an aspiring Pehelwan (wrestler) in the midst of this intense feud
